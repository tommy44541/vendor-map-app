隱私權政策

最後更新日期:2026 年 6 月 19 日
營運者:Slighter&Tom coop

本隱私權政策說明 Slighter&Tom coop(下稱「我們」)在你使用攤位雷達 app(下稱「本服務」)時,如何蒐集、使用、儲存與分享你的個人資料。使用本服務即表示你同意本政策所述之資料處理方式。

一、我們蒐集哪些資料

1. 帳號資料

  • 姓名、電子郵件地址、加密過的密碼雜湊值
  • 透過 Google Sign-In 註冊時,我們從 Google 取得你的 email、姓名與 Google 帳號識別碼
  • 帳號角色(消費者 / 商家)、店名與商家驗證資料(僅限商家)

2. 位置資料

  • 你主動授權後的裝置 GPS 經緯度,用於探索附近商家與排序結果
  • 你自行儲存的地點標籤(例如「家」「公司」)與對應地址
  • 商家用戶設定的店家位置與營業地點

3. 裝置與推播

  • FCM(Firebase Cloud Messaging)裝置 token,用於發送營業通知
  • 穩定裝置識別碼、作業系統平台(iOS / Android)、app 版本

4. 相機

  • 掃描 QR Code 訂閱商家時暫時讀取相機影像
  • 影像不會被儲存或上傳,僅在裝置端即時解碼成 merchant ID 後丟棄

5. 使用紀錄

  • 訂閱清單、收藏的商家、設定的通知範圍
  • 商家用戶發出的通知內容、地點與成功 / 失敗統計
  • 後端伺服器的請求 log(IP、時間、endpoint、status),保留 30 天供故障排查

我們不蒐集:照片或影片(除即時 QR 掃描)、通訊錄、簡訊、瀏覽紀錄、付款資訊、健康或財務資料、廣告 ID。

二、我們如何使用這些資料

  • 提供 app 核心功能:註冊登入、商家探索、訂閱通知、QR Code 訂閱、地點管理
  • 透過 Firebase Cloud Messaging 對你已訂閱的商家通知
  • 故障排查、防止濫用、改善服務
  • 必要時依法律或主管機關要求配合

我們不會將你的資料用於廣告投放,也不會將個人識別資料賣給第三方。

三、第三方服務

本服務在運作上會將部分資料傳輸至以下第三方服務提供者:

  • Google Cloud Platform(後端基礎建設、Cloud Run、Secret Manager,主要位置 asia-east1)
  • Supabase(PostgreSQL 資料庫)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging(推播服務,Google 子公司)
  • Google Sign-In / OAuth(若你選擇用 Google 登入)
  • Google Maps SDK(地圖顯示)

這些第三方各自有其隱私政策,我們會在合理範圍內確認其符合業界標準。

四、資料保存與位置

  • 主要資料儲存於 Google Cloud Platform 台灣(asia-east1)機房
  • 部分備援與災難恢復可能涉及亞洲其他區域(如 asia-northeast1 東京)
  • 帳號刪除後 30 天內清除所有可識別資料,匿名統計資料可能繼續保留

五、你的權利

你可以隨時透過聯絡信箱要求:

  • 存取我們持有的你的個人資料
  • 修正錯誤的資料
  • 刪除帳號與相關資料
  • 暫停或撤回授權(例如關閉位置權限、取消推播訂閱)
  • 取得你資料的可攜出副本

我們會於收到請求後 30 個工作天內回覆。

六、資料安全

  • 所有 API 流量強制 HTTPS / TLS 1.2+ 加密
  • 密碼以 Argon2id 雜湊儲存,從不以明文記錄
  • 機密金鑰(JWT 簽章、資料庫連線字串)儲存於 GCP Secret Manager 並走 IAM 控管
  • 後端服務以最小權限原則部署,定期檢視存取記錄

七、兒童隱私

本服務並非設計給 13 歲以下兒童使用。我們不會故意蒐集 13 歲以下兒童的個人資料。若你是家長並發現孩子向我們提供了個資,請聯絡我們刪除。

八、政策變更

我們可能不時更新本政策。重大變更會在 app 內或本頁面通知,並更新上方的「最後更新日期」。建議你定期檢視本頁面。

Privacy Policy

Applies to: Vendor Map (攤位雷達) Android application
Last updated: June 19, 2026
Operator: Slighter&Tom coop
Contact: devwang0223@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Slighter&Tom coop ("we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data when you use the Vendor Map app ("the Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the data practices described herein.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Data

  • Name, email address, hashed password
  • For Google Sign-In, we receive your email, name, and Google account identifier from Google
  • Account role (consumer / merchant), store name, and business verification info (merchants only)

1.2 Location Data

  • Device GPS coordinates (with your explicit permission) for nearby merchant discovery and result ranking
  • Location labels you save (e.g., "Home", "Office") with their addresses
  • For merchants: configured business locations and operating sites

1.3 Device & Push

  • FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) device token for delivering notifications
  • Stable device identifier, OS platform (iOS / Android), and app version

1.4 Camera

  • Camera stream is temporarily accessed when scanning a QR code to subscribe to a merchant
  • No images are stored or uploaded. Frames are decoded on-device into a merchant ID and discarded

1.5 Usage Records

  • Subscriptions, favorited merchants, notification radius preferences
  • For merchants: notification content, locations, send / failure counts
  • Backend request logs (IP, timestamp, endpoint, status) retained for 30 days for debugging

We do not collect: photos or videos (other than transient QR scanning), contacts, SMS, browsing history, payment information, health or financial data, or advertising IDs.

2. How We Use This Data

  • Provide core app functionality: registration, login, merchant discovery, push subscription, QR subscription, location management
  • Deliver notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging to subscribed users
  • Troubleshooting, abuse prevention, service improvement
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory obligations when required

We do not use your data for advertising, nor do we sell personally identifiable data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Operation of the Service involves transferring certain data to the following providers:

  • Google Cloud Platform (backend infrastructure, Cloud Run, Secret Manager, primary location asia-east1)
  • Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosting)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications, Google subsidiary)
  • Google Sign-In / OAuth (if you choose to sign in with Google)
  • Google Maps SDK (map rendering)

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We make reasonable efforts to ensure they meet industry standards.

4. Data Retention & Location

  • Primary data is stored in Google Cloud Platform's Taiwan (asia-east1) region
  • Disaster-recovery replicas may include other Asia regions such as asia-northeast1 (Tokyo)
  • Upon account deletion, identifiable data is purged within 30 days; anonymized statistics may be retained

5. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Suspend or revoke permissions (e.g., disable location, cancel push subscriptions)
  • Obtain a portable copy of your data

We will respond within 30 business days of receiving a request.

6. Data Security

  • All API traffic is enforced over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Passwords are hashed with Argon2id; plaintext is never logged
  • Secrets (JWT signing keys, database connection strings) are stored in GCP Secret Manager with IAM-controlled access
  • Backend services follow least-privilege deployment; access logs are reviewed regularly

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to delete it.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app or on this page, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
